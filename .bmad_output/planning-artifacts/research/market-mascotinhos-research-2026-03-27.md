# Market Research: Mascotinhos & AI-Generated Personalized Digital Illustrations in Brazil

**Business Context:** Automated WhatsApp bot selling AI-generated personalized children's cartoon illustrations (mascotinhos) at R$29.90/each. Lead acquisition via Meta click-to-WhatsApp ads. Planned expansion into pet avatars, professional caricatures, couple/family illustrations, and corporate mascots.

---

## SECTION 1: Target Market & Customer Segmentation

### Primary Customer Demographics

**Core Buyer Profile:**
- **Primary buyer:** Brazilian mothers, ages 25-40 (Millennials / older Gen Z), planning children's birthday parties
- **Income level:** Classes B and C — mid-range spenders who want personalization but are price-conscious
- **Education level:** Typically secondary to higher education, heavy social media users
- **Digital behavior:** 94% of Brazilians use WhatsApp; millennials represent 32% of Brazil's digital market with high intent to personalize purchases
- **Geographic concentration:** Southeast Brazil (São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Minas Gerais) leads in digital commerce, followed by South and Northeast; however, digital delivery removes geographic friction significantly

**[Source: Opinion Box – Pesquisa WhatsApp no Brasil 2025, https://materiais.opinionbox.com/pesquisa-whatsapp-no-brasil]** [HIGH]
**[Source: Midiamobi – The Brazilian Digital Consumer in 2025, https://www.midiamobi.com/en/post/o-consumidor-digital-brasileiro-em-2025-como-transformar-insights-em-resultados]** [HIGH]

### Customer Personas

**Persona 1 – "Mãe Festeira" (The Party-Planning Mom)**
- Age: 28-38, has 1-2 children ages 1-8
- Spends 3-6 months planning the child's birthday party
- Browses Instagram and TikTok for party inspiration; follows party decoration accounts
- Buys from Elo7, Mercado Livre, and Instagram sellers
- Budget: R$3,000–R$10,000 per party; willing to pay for unique personalized items
- Pain point: Generic party kits don't capture the child's personality; wants something "exclusivo"
- WhatsApp is her primary communication tool — she expects quick, responsive service
- **Conversion trigger:** Sees a beautiful mascotinho on Instagram/TikTok and wants one for her child

**Persona 2 – "Organizadora de Eventos Infantis" (Children's Event Planner)**
- Age: 25-45, professional event planner or buffet decorator
- Orders mascotinhos in bulk for multiple clients (5-20 per month)
- Price-sensitive but values speed, quality, and reliability
- Needs fast turnaround (24-48h) and consistent quality
- **Conversion trigger:** Discovers bot through colleague recommendation; becomes recurring B2B buyer

**Persona 3 – "Empreendedora de Kit Festa" (Party Kit Seller)**
- Age: 22-40, sells personalized party kits on Elo7, Mercado Livre, or Instagram
- Uses mascotinhos as a component of larger party design packages
- Buys wholesale or at discount; high LTV customer
- **Conversion trigger:** Meta ad targeting small business owners offering party supplies

**Persona 4 – "Presenteadora Digital" (Digital Gift Giver)**
- Age: 25-50, buys mascotinhos as unique gifts for children (nieces, nephews, godchildren)
- Lower frequency purchase (1-2x/year) but impulsive buyer triggered by birthdays
- Attracted by the novelty and shareability of the illustration on social media

### Customer Journey & Buying Behavior

```
Discovery → Consideration → Purchase → Delivery → Advocacy
```

1. **Discovery:** Meta click-to-WhatsApp ad (scrolling Instagram/Facebook reels showing a mascotinho being created)
2. **Consideration:** Arrives on WhatsApp bot; views sample portfolio; price confirmed (R$29.90); friction is minimal
3. **Purchase:** Bot collects photo + preferences; payment via PIX (dominant in Brazil since 2025) or credit card
4. **Delivery:** Illustration delivered via WhatsApp in 24-48h (digital, instant gratification)
5. **Advocacy:** Customer shares mascotinho on WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories → organic viral loop

**Key behavioral insight:** 67% of Brazilian parents spend more than they planned on children's birthdays (Datafolha 2023). The emotional nature of the purchase (child's party) reduces price sensitivity significantly. **[Source: Datafolha survey cited in Hapy.com.br party cost guide, https://hapy.com.br/blog/detalhe/quanto-custa-festa-infantil]** [HIGH]

### Price Sensitivity Analysis

- At R$29.90, the mascotinho represents ~0.3-0.5% of a mid-range party budget (R$6,000-R$10,000)
- Comparable to a single personalized lembrancinha item (avg R$15-30 per unit)
- At this price point, the purchase is essentially **impulse-buy territory** for target consumers
- The "perceived value" of a fully personalized illustration significantly exceeds its price; this is a key marketing leverage point
- Research confirms 67% of Brazilian consumers choose stores offering personalized experiences — premium willingness to pay exists [MEDIUM]
