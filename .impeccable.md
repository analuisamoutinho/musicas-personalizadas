# Mascotinhos — Design Context

## Users
Brazilian mothers ages 25-40 (classes B and C), heavy WhatsApp and social media users, planning children's birthday parties ("festa infantil"). They spend R$3,000-R$10,000 per party and are emotionally invested in their child's celebration. They seek hyper-personalization and exclusivity ("algo exclusivo para meu filho"). Non-technical — they want convenience over DIY. Secondary users include event planners (B2B) and digital gift givers (aunts, uncles, godparents).

**Context of use:** Mobile-first (95%+ traffic from Meta ads on mobile). WhatsApp is the primary channel. The web experience serves as a portfolio/landing page to build trust before the WhatsApp conversation begins.

**Job to be done:** Transform a photo of their child into a personalized, professional-quality illustration for birthday party materials (invites, cake toppers, labels, banners) — fast, easy, and affordable.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice:** Warm, empathetic, casual Brazilian Portuguese. Emoji-rich. Like a caring friend, not a tech company.
- **Tone:** Celebratory, joyful, maternal. "Feito com carinho" (made with care).
- **3-word personality:** Joyful, Caring, Magical.
- **Emotional goals:** Pride (child's uniqueness captured), Care (feels attended to), Trust (professional quality), Ease (no friction), Joy (party energy).

## Aesthetic Direction — "Festa" (The Joyful Curator)

**Visual tone:** Celebratory editorial — treats every screen like a premium, physical invitation. Vibrant but not overwhelming. Premium-but-playful. Party energy meets polished craft.

**Style:** Soft-vivid dichotomy with confetti accents, gradient CTAs, and editorial typography. No harsh borders — depth through tonal layering. Rounded corners (16px minimum). Asymmetric layouts for energy.

### Key Design Rules

1. **No 1px borders for sectioning.** Use tonal shifts between surface tokens to define boundaries.
2. **No pure black (#000).** Always use `on-surface` (#352D2F) for warmth.
3. **No 90° corners.** Minimum 16px border-radius. Buttons use `rounded-full`.
4. **No flat drop shadows.** Shadows must be tinted with primary color and hyper-diffused.
5. **Glassmorphism for overlays:** Semi-transparent white + 12-20px backdrop-blur for floating nav, modals.
6. **Confetti offset:** Every third card features a small tertiary star/circle overlapping the top-right corner.
7. **Gradient CTAs:** Primary buttons always use the pink gradient, never flat color.
8. **Asymmetric margins for editorial feel.** Not everything centered — intentional off-balance creates energy.

### Interaction & Motion

- Buttons: Scale 98% on press, smooth 200ms ease-out transitions.
- Cards: Hover shadow expansion (subtle glow).
- Touch targets: Minimum 44x44px.
- WhatsApp floating button: Fixed bottom-right, green #25D366, scale on hover.
- Celebration progress: Staggered circles/stars that "light up" in tertiary yellow.

### References & Anti-References

**References:**
- @studio.convites Instagram ads (warm cream, 3D chibi on pedestal, bold typography)- Brazilian festa infantil Pinterest aesthetic

**Anti-references (do NOT look like):**
- Generic SaaS/tech product pages (cold, blue, corporate)
- Dark mode developer tools
- Flat, template-y party planning apps
- ChatGPT's default UI
- Anything that feels robotic or impersonal

### Accessibility

- Text contrast minimum 4.5:1 against surface tokens.
- Focus states: 2px outer glow in `secondary-fixed` (#B1D5FF).
- Error states: `error` (#B41340) text + `error-container` (#F74B6D) at 20% opacity background.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — reduce confetti animation.
- Touch targets minimum 44px.
- Responsive breakpoints: 375px (phone), 768px (tablet), 1024px (desktop), 1440px (wide).

## Design Principles

1. **Joy First** — Every interaction should feel like a celebration. The interface should evoke the same excitement as planning a festa infantil.
2. **Trust Through Craft** — Premium visual quality (gradients, depth, typography) builds the confidence to pay R$29.90 on impulse. Before/after portfolio is the primary trust mechanism.
3. **Warmth Over Efficiency** — The brand is a caring friend, not a vending machine. Intentional artificial delays ("estou preparando com carinho") create perceived production value.
4. **Mobile-Native Simplicity** — Single-column, touch-friendly, no clutter. Respect WhatsApp as the primary medium. Quick-reply patterns over complex forms.
5. **Celebrate the Child** — The mascotinho illustration is always the hero. Large, prominent, and lovingly framed. Everything else supports it.
